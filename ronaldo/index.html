Cristiano Ronaldo

One of the greatest footballers of all time

Biography

Cristiano Ronaldo dos Santos Aveiro, commonly known as Cristiano Ronaldo, is a Portuguese professional footballer who was born on February 5, 1985, in Funchal, Madeira, Portugal. He is widely regarded as one of the greatest footballers of all time.

Ronaldo has played for several top clubs throughout his career, including Sporting CP, Manchester United, Real Madrid, Juventus, and as of 2021, he returned to Manchester United.

He has won numerous awards and accolades, including multiple FIFA Ballon d'Or awards, which are given to the best player in the world.

Achievements

  • 5-time Ballon d'Or winner
  • 4-time European Golden Shoe winner
  • 5-time UEFA Champions League winner
  • Multiple domestic league titles
  • Record for most goals scored in UEFA Champions League history
